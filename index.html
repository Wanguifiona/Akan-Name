Akan Name repo generator

Hey there! This web application is designed to derive Akan names from your birthday details using the Ghanian culture for children naming. In Ghananian culture childeren are given names as 'day names' corresponding to the day of the week they were born.

Fill the form below and it will calculate for the you the day of the week you were born and depending on your gender you will get a Akan name
